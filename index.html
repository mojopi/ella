Once there was a leaf named Lucky. He loved his life in his tree with his family and friends.
One day, a HUGE gust of wind hit the tree and Lucky fell out!
Lucky began to cry because he was so sad. He wanted to go back to his family.
To make himself feel better, Lucky decided to explore the city. First, he went to the Empire State Building.
Next he went to Time Square to see the lights and people.
Then he went to the subway to roam around the city.
Lucky got sad because he missed his tree and family. So he decided to go to the park.
When Lucky went to the park, he met some new friends name Timmy and Lucy. Timmy and Lucy took Lucky in and they all became best friends.
Though Lucky missed his family, he found a new family with his friends. The end.
